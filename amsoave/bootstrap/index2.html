Welcome to AHB Tooling & Machinery, Inc. - Complete Metal Working Solutions

AHB Tooling & Machinery, Inc. is an industrial distributor specializing in abrasives, bandsaw blades, cutting tools, fluids, machinery, and waterjet supplies. With an expanded customer service department and same day shipping capability, our company is poised to fill many of your shop's needs for metal removal. AHB Tooling & Machinery's field and internal sales staff stays continually trained in all technical areas to provide our customers the latest in cost saving applications.

AHB Tooling & Machinery, Inc. believes there is a need to service customers "at the spindle". Larger catalog houses generally cannot provide this level of service. We guarantee to save your company money by offering you top quality products at competitive pricing. Our goal is to reduce your machining costs. Use our online ordering feature or call us today for a no risk trial order.

In order to serve you better, AHB Tooling & Machinery, Inc. has become ISO 9001:2000 Registered. We believe that by obtaining ISO certification, AHB Tooling & Machinery, Inc, will be a more effective company, and help us to provide your company with metalworking solutions that will impact your bottom line. With locations in Roseville, Saginaw and Jackson, we provide local service to the entire Lower Michigan.

At Your Service

AHB Tooling & Machinery, Inc., is a "full service supplier" supplying customers with a full range of saw blades, abrasives, tooling, coolants, cutting tools, fluids and even spray paints, work gloves and safety supplies. We can inventory these products at your location on a regular schedule keeping your costs of inventory down and more importantly, not allowing you to run out of those required supplies!

AHB Tooling & Machinery, Inc., promotes both ToolBoss and CribMaster vending machines to secure and accurately maintain your inventory. Our delivery personnel can be equipped with bar code scanners to check inventory status. The inventory information will be immediately downloaded to our inventory system. Our warehouse personnel will then pull the replenishment stock and have the product ready for shipment or delivery within one day.

As you can see, our inventory management solutions will provide you with quick and accurate replenishment on your products. Have an AHB Tooling & Machinery, Inc., account representative tell you how this important service can save your company from tying up costly inventory dollars!
