Welcome to AHB Tooling & Machinery, Inc.

AHB Tooling & Machinery, Inc. is an industrial distributor specializing in abrasives, bandsaw blades, cutting tools, fluids, machinery, and waterjet supplies. With an expanded customer service department and same day shipping capability, our company is poised to fill many of your shop's needs for metal removal. AHB Tooling & Machinery's field and internal sales staff stays continually trained in all technical areas to provide our customers the latest in cost saving applications. AHB Tooling & Machinery, Inc. believes there is a need to service customers "at the spindle". Larger catalog houses generally cannot provide this level of service. We guarantee to save your company money by offering you top quality products at competitive pricing. Our goal is to reduce your machining costs. Use our online ordering feature or call us today for a no risk trial order. In order to serve you better, AHB Tooling & Machinery, Inc. has become ISO 9001:2000 Registered. We believe that by obtaining ISO certification, AHB Tooling & Machinery, Inc, will be a more effective company, and help us to provide your company with metalworking solutions that will impact your bottom line. With locations in Roseville, Saginaw and Jackson, we provide local service to the entire Lower Michigan.
